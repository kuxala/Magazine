A Brief History

Of Mars

  • 2027 - First Landing

    Starship, a marvel of engineering, embarked on an odyssey across the cosmic ocean, bridging the gap between Earth and Mars. With its sleek stainless-steel exterior glinting in the cosmic sunlight, the vessel navigated the vast expanse of space, a symbol of human determination and technological prowess. The journey among the stars was both a triumph of innovation and a testament to the collective spirit that propelled humanity beyond the confines of Earth.

  • 2028 - First Colony

    In the year 2028, a diverse group of visionaries, scientists, and adventurers embarked on an unprecedented journey to Mars. Their mission: to establish the first human colony on the Red Planet. Named Project Olympus, after the largest volcano in the solar system, the colony aimed to redefine the possibilities of human habitation beyond Earth.

  • 2030 - First "Home"
